About Flyhub AirLine

Here's a compilation of all the travel queries you may have. We're pretty sure the answer to your question will be here. Just in case you don't see it, please use contact us option mentioned below and we will get back to you for resolution.

  • flyhub(Le Travenues Technology Ltd.) was started by Rajnish Kumar and Aloke Bajpai in June 2007. flyhub is an intelligent, AI-based travel app, with a user base of over 170 million travellers. flyhub uses Artificial Intelligence for deal discovery, personalized recommendations, dynamic marketing, airfare predictions, train delay information, PNR confirmation status, infrastructure cost optimization, business intelligence and for providing fully-automated customer service over chat and voice.

  • Know more about the flyhub mobile apps here: https://www.flyhub.com/mobile

  • We take the privacy of our users very seriously! You can view our Privacy Policy: https://www.flyhub.com/about/more-info/privacy/ & Terms Of Use: https://www.flyhub.com/about/more-info/terms-of-use/.

  • flyhub is an intelligent, AI-based travel platform that helps you organise, book and track your trips. With unique features such as the Online/Offline Train Running Status, PNR Prediction, Refund Calculator, TDR tracking, Station Alarm and so on, the flyhub Trains app offers a seamless booking experience. The app can currently be used in 8 Indian languages. On the other hand, the flyhub flights app also offers the users some unique features such as fare prediction, auto web check-in and so on!

  • In case you have a specific question not addressed in this section then, please reach out to us by clicking here: https://www.flyhub.com/help-center#trip-list.

  • For any feedback or queries, you can visit the flyhub customer service section: https://flyhub.to/helpcenter You could also reach out to us via social media: Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/flyhub Twitter: https://twitter.com/flyhub Instagram: https://www.instagram.com/flyhub

  • Please follow the steps mentioned below to track your refund status: 1. Log in to your flyhub account 2. Click on the customer service tab 3. Select your trip from the 'MyTrips' section 4. Click on the booking ID and check the refund status

  • Please follow these simple steps to cancel your ticket on flyhub: - Log in to flyhub - Visit the 'My Trips' section on the app - Select the desired booking, scroll down and click on the 'cancel ticket' tab to initiate cancellation. - In case of partial cancellation, please select the desired passenger(s) and click on the cancel tab.

  • For refund of a confirmed booking, you need to place a cancellation request from the My Trips section of the flyhub app. For refund related to a failed booking, the amount deducted from your account will be reversed in the source account within 7 days
