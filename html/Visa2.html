Documents Required

Documents Required for UAE Tourist visa

Photograph

  • Upload scanned colour copy of your passport-sized photograph.
  • Photo should be taken against a white background.
  • Dimensions should be 45mm height x 35mm width.

Passport

  • Upload coloured copies of the front and last page of your passport.
  • Ensure that passport should be valid for 6 months from the date of entry to the UAE.

Pan Card

  • Applicant need to upload scanned copy of their pan card. (If you are student or minor traveleing with parent/guardian then provide their pan card)
  • All Indian passport holders require a valid visa to enter UAE.
  • You need to be fully vaccinated with Covishield or any of the EMA-approved vaccines (Pfizer, Moderna, AstraZeneca, Covishield) to travel to UAE.
  • Each applicant must have all the mandatory documents as mentioned in the document requirement section.
  • Fee may vary for the country if the rate of the embassy fluctuates.
  • Processing time is indicative & prices are subject to fluctuation.
  • Entry & Exit port should be Dubai airport for transit visa.
  • The decision to grant or refuse a visa is the sole prerogative of the Government of UAE.
